Explanation

In this hands-on lab, experiment with the maximum and minimum values for the y-axis. Notice how different decisions about the y-axis range create differences of appearance with the graph. You can choose to tell a certain story, or emphasize a certain relation, depending on how you confine your axis.

Let this serve as a demonstration for how graphical presentations can impact your perception of the statistical facts!
