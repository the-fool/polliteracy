Welcome!

Welcome to this learning module on basic poll literacy!

Our goal is to get equipped to tackle the wild world of public opinion polls with confidence, wisdom, and critical analytical skills. Polls are an important part of our modern-day political activity, and the more you understand how they work, the better you can make informed opinions about social issues.

There are so many polls, we need to know which ones deserve our trust. By the end of this module you will be able to make good decisions about whether a poll is trustworthy. In order to gain this ability, we will examine how large-scale polls work, and what methods are used to gather the data.
