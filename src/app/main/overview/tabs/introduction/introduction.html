Welcome!

Welcome to this learning module on poll literacy! Our goal is to get equipped to tackle the wild world of public opinion polls with confidence, wisdom, and critical analytical skills. An ability to maneuver through the present-day barrage of polls is becoming more and more important, as polls take more of center stage in our political climate, become more mathematically rigorous, and come under the sway of dishonest special interest groups.

By the end of this module, you will be able to take a critical stance against news reports of poll results, as well as understand the underlying methodology for how those poll results came to be.
